Runner Up / Dublin Student Startup Weekend
January 2020

In 54 hours my team developed Studio. Studio was created to solve two issues. Firstly; students may pick up skills through their college course but are not trusted as employees to use these skills as they don't have much work experience. Secondly, businesses may want to leverage skilled professions such as web design, application development or photography to help their business, but cannot afford to pay highly skilled professionals. Studio is a platform to link students with businesses to solve these issues. Studio vets quality to provide value to the businesses, while also managing the relationship to give value to the student.
